Welcome to Horse Grade GitHub Page.

Horse Grade is an app which will compare winning times against track record and will grade performance. The closest to 100% or greater, the better the performance. You can test app by clicking the link. Click Here

Design

This was made using Sencha Architect and was developed just for fun.

Notes

The formula used is Record/(Win+ (Loss * 1/5))*100

Record - Track record for distance race
Win - Winning Time of race
Loss - Length loss by (note: if horse won this is left blank)
Track record time of 2:01.4 at PIMLICO for 1 1/4 miles was by Horse name Manzotti; Values for App: ORB's first entry Race 12 at PIMLICO at 1 1/4 mile Win = 202.89 Loss = 0 Record = 201.4 OXBOW's first entry Race 12 at PIMLICO at 1 1/4 mile Win = 202.89 Loss = 9.5 Record = 201.4 Note : This was taken straight from racing form from PIMLICO. ORB's first entry which was the results from Kentucky Derby was 2:0289. Notice the colon on time from race is left out and the superscript is entered as a decimal. I found track records from PIMLICO here : Http://www.pimlico.com/race-info/track-records
